Explore the Universe with AstroWorld

AstroWorld is your personal planetarium and celestial guide, bringing the wonders of the night sky 
to your fingertips. Whether you’re a casual stargazer or a dedicated astronomer, AstroWorld offers 
an immersive experience tailored to your location and time.

Key Features:

• Real-time Sky Map – Visualize the night sky exactly as it appears from your location

• Location-Based Viewing – Automatically detect or manually set your position on Earth

• Star and Planet Search – Easily find and learn about stars, planets, and celestial objects

• In-Depth Information – Explore detailed data like spectral types, magnitudes, and coordinates

• Time-Sensitive Accuracy – Watch the sky shift as the hours pass with real-time updates

Learn and Discover:

AstroWorld is more than a visual tool. It helps you understand the sky above. 
Access a built-in glossary to learn about astronomical concepts such as magnitudes, spectral types, 
right ascension, and declination.

Easy to Use:

1. Allow location access or set your location manually

2. Navigate the sky with intuitive pan and zoom controls

3. Tap on stars or planets to view detailed information

4. Use the search feature to find specific celestial objects

Powered by Precision:

AstroWorld uses data from the HYG Catalogue and the Skyfield Python library to ensure accuracy and 
reliability.
